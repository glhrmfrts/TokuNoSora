### Controls

Keyboard:
  Arrows up/down - Accelerate/break
  Arrows left/right - Steer
  Space - Jump
  Esc - Pause

XBOX 360 Controller:
  RB/LB - Accelerate/break
  Left stick - Steer
  A - Jump
  Start - Pause
